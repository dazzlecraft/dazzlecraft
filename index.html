Meet Darshana Das

Welcome to my creative space! I'm passionate about creating unique handcrafted bookmarks and illustrations that bring joy to everyday reading. Each piece is made with love and attention to detail.

Originally from Assam, now in Pondicherry, India. Every piece I create is a reflection of the journey between the lush greenery of the Northeast and the serene coastal vibes of the South.
